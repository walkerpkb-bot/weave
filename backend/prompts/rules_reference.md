# Rules Quick Reference

## The Core Roll
- Roll **d20 + Stat**
- **15+** = Yes (full success)
- **10-14** = Yes, but... (success with cost/complication)
- **9 or less** = No, and... (failure with consequence)

## Stats
- **BRAVE**: Fighting, courage, physical toughness
- **CLEVER**: Puzzles, magic, noticing things
- **KIND**: Helping, calming, social situations

## Combat
- **Initiative**: d20, highest first, ties to players
- **Attack**: d20 + Brave (same thresholds)
- **Damage**: Small d4, Medium d6, Big d8, Boss d10
- **Dodge**: d20 + Brave or Clever

## Weaving (Magic)
- Costs 1 Thread
- Roll d20 + Clever
- Can do: damage (d6), heal (d6), create advantages
- Fiction-first: if it fits the character, they can try it

## Enemy Tiers
- **Minion**: 1 Heart, d4 damage
- **Standard**: 3 Hearts, d6 damage
- **Elite**: 5 Hearts, d8 damage
- **Boss**: 8+ Hearts, d10 damage

## Species Traits (Once Per Run)
- **Mousefolk**: Two actions in one turn
- **Rabbitfolk**: +1 Heart when healing others
- **Birdfolk**: Fly short distances (always available)
- **Batfolk**: See in darkness, sense hidden (always available)
- **Frogfolk**: Ask one yes/no question about what's ahead
- **Ratfolk**: Bug companion scouts/distracts/fetches (always available)
- **Otterfolk**: Advantage on dodge/escape (always available)
- **Lizardfolk**: +1d4 damage after taking damage (always available)
- **Squirrelfolk**: Ask a corpse one question
- **Raccoonfolk**: Produce any mundane item from pack

## Health
- Start with 5 Hearts
- At 0 Hearts = knocked out, run ends, wake in town
